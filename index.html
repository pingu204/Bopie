How are these made?

As an avid The Sims™️ 4 player, I picked up the hobby of manipulating in-game screenshots and transforming them into photoshoots or stills.


The core of renders relies on the game's pose player mod, which is used to pose and/or animate sims in the game. Perhaps the bulk of beautifying these renders goes to the post-processing, in which I use Adobe Photoshop. Meanwhile, I use CapCut in editing videos.
